## 简体中文

### 简介

本工具依据中华人民共和国邮政行业标准《国内给据邮件条码》（YZ/T 0126-2006）开发。

可用于中国邮政给据函件条码的生成与校验，并提供最近生成条码记录功能，避免因忘记上次生成到哪个号码，而在邮电局倒闭时陷入条码重复的尴尬局面。

### 功能

- 按照 YZ/T 0126-2006 标准生成给据函件条码
- 校验给据函件条码的有效性
- 自动记录最近生成的条码

## English

### Introduction

This tool is developed in accordance with the postal industry standard of the People's Republic of China, **Barcode for Domestic recorded item  (YZ/T 0126-2006)**.

It can be used to generate and validate China Post registered mail barcodes. The tool also keeps track of the most recently generated barcode, helping prevent the embarrassing situation of duplicate barcodes when you deliberately deviate from the standard procedures at the post office.

### Features

- Generate registered mail barcodes compliant with YZ/T 0126-2006
- Validate registered mail barcode numbers
- Keep a record of the most recently generated barcode
